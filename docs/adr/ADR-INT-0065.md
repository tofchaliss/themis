# ADR-INT-0065: Intelligence Quality Shall Be Continuously Evaluated

Status

Accepted

Category

Evaluation

Decision

Enterprise intelligence capabilities shall undergo continuous evaluation to measure accuracy, consistency, latency,
cost, and business usefulness.

Evaluation shall influence provider selection but shall never modify enterprise truth.

Context

Intelligence systems evolve continuously.

Provider performance changes over time.

Enterprise expectations also evolve.

Problem Statement

How can the enterprise ensure long-term intelligence quality?

Decision

Every capability shall define measurable evaluation criteria including:

- accuracy
- consistency
- latency
- cost
- proposal acceptance rate
- human feedback
- business impact

Evaluation results become operational intelligence rather than business knowledge.

Rationale

Continuous evaluation enables:

- provider optimization
- quality improvement
- enterprise confidence

Alternatives Considered

Periodic manual review only.

Rejected.

Provider benchmarks only.

Rejected.

Consequences

Positive

- Better capability selection.
- Continuous improvement.
- Evidence-based optimization.

Negative

- Evaluation infrastructure required.

Implementation Impact

Evaluation services operate independently from business workflows.
