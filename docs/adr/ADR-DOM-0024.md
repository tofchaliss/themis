# ADR-DOM-0024: Proposal Lifecycle Precedes Enterprise Position

Status

Accepted

Category

Governance

Decision

Every recommendation, assessment, AI suggestion, enrichment, or human analysis shall enter Governance as a Proposal
before becoming an Enterprise Position.

No Proposal is authoritative.

Context

Enterprise decisions originate from many independent sources.

These inputs require enterprise evaluation before becoming official decisions.

Problem Statement

How does enterprise reasoning transition from recommendation to authoritative truth?

Decision

The Proposal lifecycle is:

Proposal

↓

Evaluation

↓

Acceptance or Rejection

↓

Enterprise Position

Only accepted Proposals influence enterprise truth.

Historical Proposals remain part of enterprise history.

Rationale

This lifecycle separates reasoning from authority.

It also enables AI and future automation without granting decision authority.

Alternatives Considered

Direct Enterprise Position creation

Rejected.

Enterprise truth requires governance.

Consequences

Positive

- Explainable reasoning.
- AI compatibility.
- Controlled governance.

Negative

- Additional workflow.

Implementation Impact

Governance workflows shall preserve Proposal history independently of Enterprise Positions.
